BDSL LAB

Pune institute of Computer Technology

Blockchain and Distributed Systems Security Lab hosts many activities such as projects, consultancy, webinars etc. The focus of the lab is to conduct the activities related to distributed system, security and blockchain. Blockchain is emerging technology and used widely in banking, healthcare and government sectors.

Learn more
Aims and Objectives of BDSL

  • To create an environment and raise awareness among developers and society by educating them in the Blockchain and Distributed system technology.
  • To identify and explore the use cases of the blockchain technology
  • To design and development of applications using blockchain technology
  • To identify the research issues and gaps in blockchain infrastructure and providing the solutions.
  • To carry out industry sponsored projects
  • To take consultancy projects,
  • To carry product development activities,
  • To conduct faculty development programs
  • To give opportunity to students for internships,
  • To collaborate with national and international partners
